E-Signature Disclosure

Electronic Signature Consent

By signing a document electronically through Get Signatures, you consent to:

  • Conduct the transaction electronically
  • Use electronic signatures instead of handwritten signatures
  • Receive documents and notices electronically

Legal Effect

Under the US Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (ESIGN) and state Uniform Electronic Transactions Acts (UETA), electronic signatures have the same legal effect as handwritten signatures in most circumstances.

Important: Some documents cannot be signed electronically, including wills, family law documents, and certain real estate documents. Check your local laws.

Withdrawing Consent

You may withdraw your consent to electronic signatures at any time by declining to sign. This will not affect any signatures already provided.

Hardware/Software Requirements

To use the Service, you need:

  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Internet connection
  • Email account to receive signing invitations

Security Practices

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Your documents are encrypted in your browser using AES-256-GCM before being uploaded. The encryption key never leaves your device. We cannot read your documents.

Infrastructure Security

  • Hosting: Cloudflare Workers (edge computing)
  • Data Storage: Cloudflare KV with encryption at rest
  • Transport: TLS 1.3 encryption for all connections
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing (Argon2)

Session Security

  • Signing links expire after 7 days by default
  • Each recipient gets a unique, non-guessable signing link
  • Sessions are deleted after completion or expiration

Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability, please email us at bobamatchasolutions@gmail.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure.
